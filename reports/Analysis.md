# Loan Approval Prediction Analysis

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains borrower information such as age, income, employment length and credit history.

## Key Insights
- Higher income increases loan approval probability
- Loan percent income strongly affects approval
- Credit history length plays an important role

## Model Performance
Logistic Regression Accuracy: 84%

## Explainability Insights
SHAP analysis shows income and credit history are the most influential features.
